Overview

Kalexiko is an in-house digital marketing company based in Birmingham UK. We are a small and personal team based in our 850-square-foot studio with a view of a formidable Birmingham skyline. This is where we make our vision a reality.

Since we started in 2006, our ambition to create outstanding websites for organisations has enabled us to establish a foundation of strong relationships with many businesses and service providers.

Our passion is using our creative expertise and unique rapport to connect people, brands and organisations with innovative and exhilarating digital solutions.

Our greatest success has always been making uniquedigital marketing solutions work for our clients. We are constantly exploring new ground and devising new methods to drive our clients forward in their business profits and to remain competitive in reaching their audience.

As well as a complete in-house web development team we have designers with flair and digital marketing strategists, making us a fully comprehensive team. The full package if you will.
